Mandarines

"Citrus Reticula" also known as "Kinnow" is grown under natural conditions. The Kinnow is sun-ripened on the trees before it is carefully hand picked at precisely the right time.

The Kinnow has a high brix level and 12-18 seeds per fruit. The season starts at the beginning of December through to Mid March.

The fruit is first selected by hand at the orchards. Once seperated between Grade A, B and C, the Grade A product is then passed through our processing machine.

During the processing the fruit is washed, waxed and sized. There are multiple quality control officers after each process to ensure only the best fruit is packed.

This brand is our highest quality. It can be found in Russia, Ukraine and all over the Gulf States.

It comes in 10kg size.

Caliber: 48, 54, 60, 66, 72, 80, 90, 100, 110
