Pakistani Potatoes

Over the years, potato has become an important crop for both farmers and consumers in Pakistan. It is the fourth biggest crop in volume of production, it is high yielding , having a high nutritive value and gives high returns to farmers.

Pakistan is self sufficient in potatoes for household consumption and ranks in the worlds top 20 potato producing nations.

We have various types of potatoes in two catagories; White skin potato (Diamant, Ajax, Pratones, Multa, Sanate) and Red Skin Potatoes (Desiree, Cardinal, Ultamis, Lala Faisal and Raja Symphonia).

The Potatoes are packed in various way depending on our target market and customer needs. We pack in mesh bags, jute bags, pp woven bags.

Packing in 5/10/15/20/25/50KG bags.
