Grapes

We are one of the leading exporters of Indian grapes to Europe, Russia Middle and Far East. Indian grapes are available in Varieties as Thompson seedless, Flame seedless and Black (Sharad) Seedless. The availability of grapes in India is from January to April, with some early colored varieties also available in December.

We export grapes that are certified Globalgap and pack them at our HACCP Accredited pack house. Our residue limits meet the strict EU norms and are continuously monitored to meet with their perennially upgraded stringent levels. With regard to food safety 100% traceability is ensured and every cartons carry traceability codes.

Kinnow

"Citrus Reticula" also known as "Kinnow" is grown under natural conditions. The Kinnow is sun-ripened on the trees before it is carefully hand picked at precisely the right time.

The Kinnow has a high brix level and 12-18 seeds per fruit. The season starts at the beginning of December through to Mid March.

The fruit is first selected by hand at the orchards. Once seperated between Grade A, B and C, the Grade A product is then passed through our processing machine.

During the processing the fruit is washed, waxed and sized. There are multiple quality control officers after each process to ensure only the best fruit is packed.

Mangoes

The fruit markets of Pakistan refer to mango as the "Baadshah" (King). It is the most prestigous fruit of our nation and is one of the most popular Pakistan Fruits in the world.

The king of fruits is grown in both Punjab and Sindh provinces of Pakistan. We export mangoes with a delicious, mouth watering flavor with rich aroma in different varieties.

A rip mango contains 15% suger, 1% protein and signifcant amounts of Vitamen A, B & C.
